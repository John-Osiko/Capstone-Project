Who we are

The kick with hope initiative formerly known as the young achievers football is a community based organization (CBO) was founded by Sabina Gicuku and started by a group of 10 visionar and passionate youthful individuals from Korogocho slum in the year 2012 and was formed as a result of rising crime and increase in street children posing security threat to residents of Korogocho slum and its environs.

The project works with street children under the age of 17 by using the popular sport of football with the goal of empowering and nurturing talents and also engaging them in other programs such mentoring, feeding program, counselling and life skills training so as to enable them to be good example in the community and beyond.

In response to the plight of young street children. There are about 150 street children (8yrs-15yrs) in Korogocho slum. The first activity was a street families soccer tournament (5-A-Side) dubbed “kicking It for the street for street family” whereby well wishers donated clothing, food medical checkup services and also talents were spotted and enrolled into academies, taken to rehabilitation centers and some were enrolled into government schools. This led to creation of a fully functional football team for the street children being trained by two coaches 3 times a week and a match every weekend.
